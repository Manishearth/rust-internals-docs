Creates a MemCategorizationContext during type inference. This is used during upvar analysis and a few other places. Because the typeck tables are not yet complete, the results from the analysis must be used with caution:

  • rvalue promotions are not known, so the lifetimes of temporaries may be overly conservative;
  • similarly, as the results of upvar analysis are not yet known, the results around upvar accesses may be incorrect.
